# Owner Content Intake

This intake is repo-local documentation only. It does not approve deployment,
does not deploy anything, does not connect providers, and does not create or
fill environment files.

Use this document to collect owner-supplied content requirements before any
future launch decision. Unknowns must remain marked as `Owner input required`;
do not invent contact details, business hours, addresses, client names,
testimonials, certifications, awards, legal claims, guarantees, or production
policies.

## Intake Rules

- Record only owner-supplied facts or repo-approved copy.
- Mark unknown facts as `Owner input required`.
- Keep draft notes out of public runtime surfaces until separately approved.
- Keep launch and deployment decisions separate from content collection.
- Public launch cannot proceed until required owner content and explicit
  deployment approval are both supplied.
- Owner review can continue without deployment.
