# Owner-Review Rehearsal Runbook

This runbook is repo-local, template-only, non-live, and not evidence. It prepares a future owner walkthrough without filling review outcomes.

No owner feedback is recorded. No owner sign-off is recorded. No deployment approval is granted. No preview/production evidence is created.

## Rehearsal boundaries

- Show local public routes and protected admin surfaces only.
- Do not claim owner approval, live readiness, preview readiness, production readiness, deployment approval, or operational facts not supplied by the owner.
- Keep missing facts as placeholders.
- Keep admin workflow details protected.
