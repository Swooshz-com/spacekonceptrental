## Safe handoff language

Use draft, readiness, placeholder, intended, and theoretical wording only. Avoid finality wording such as handed off, sent, disclosed, approved, accepted, closed, completed, verified, cleared, evidence recorded, production checked, owner signed off, audit delivered, archive created, retention applied, or maintenance completed unless clearly negated as not allowed.

## Rental/enquiry wording boundary

Use listing, rental, event furniture, quote, enquiry, request, admin review, and owner readiness wording only. Do not use ecommerce, cart, checkout, order, payment, purchase, booking, reservation, fulfilment, or stock-reservation language.
