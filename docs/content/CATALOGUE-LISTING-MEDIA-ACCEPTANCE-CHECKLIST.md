# Catalogue Listing Media Acceptance Checklist

This checklist is repo-local, template-only, non-live, and not evidence. It is
for local review of catalogue, listing, category, event-use, protected admin
content operations, and media/alt-text boundaries only.

Do not record owner feedback, owner sign-off, production evidence, preview
evidence, deployment evidence, real business facts, or filled acceptance
results in this file.

## Media And Alt-Text Expectations

- Alt text should describe the listing image for public rental browsing.
- Fallback media wording must be clear that the image is representative or that
  media can be confirmed during enquiry follow-up.
- Media checks must not claim real availability, guarantee, certification,
  award, legal proof, service area, business hours, or contact details unless
  separately supplied and approved later.

## Public Allowed Wording

Allowed public wording includes listing, listings, enquiry, quote, request,
rental, furniture rental, event furniture, category, event-use guidance, setup
notes, rental unit, quantities, alternatives, venue, timing, and follow-up.

## Public Forbidden Wording

Forbidden public wording includes protected admin review wording, internal
notes, local acceptance status, local release-candidate language, owner handoff
language, deployment approval language, deployment firewall language, customer
account wording, public tracking wording, uploads wording, notification or CRM
wording, checkout wording, order wording, payment wording, purchase wording,
booking wording, reservation wording, fulfilment wording, and retail-flow
wording.

## Admin-Only Boundary

Protected admin content-operation guidance may mention readiness, protected
admin state, local templates, media checks, alt-text review, and deployment
approval separation. Public catalogue, listing, category, and event routes must
not expose those admin-only details.
