# Local Owner Feedback Intake Readiness

Status: repo-local, template-only, non-live owner feedback intake readiness package.

Evidence status: [NOT EVIDENCE / NOT RECORDED]

Deployment status: [DEPLOYMENT APPROVAL: NOT GRANTED]

This note prepares a future local workflow for collecting owner comments after a separate owner review is actually performed. It does not record real owner feedback, owner decisions, owner corrections, owner answers, owner approval, owner acceptance, owner rejection, owner sign-off, preview evidence, production evidence, launch clearance, or deployment permission.

## Safe future feedback buckets

Future owner comments may be classified into one of these buckets after the raw owner comment is captured separately in an approved review record:

1. Public copy correction - wording for public rental/event furniture browsing or enquiry pages that can be adjusted without adding unsupported claims.
2. Listing/category/media facts - owner-supplied facts about listing names, categories, rental units, event-use context, media state, or visible listing descriptions.
3. Image/alt-text selection - owner-supplied image preference or alt-text wording for existing listing media only.
4. Quote/enquiry wording - request, quote, enquiry, receipt, and follow-up expectation wording that remains non-promissory.
5. Admin workflow wording - protected admin labels, helper copy, and review guidance that stays admin-only.
6. Missing contact/service-area/policy details - facts that remain absent until the owner supplies them explicitly.
7. Claims blocked until supplied - awards, certifications, guarantees, legal claims, client names, testimonials, service-area promises, response-time claims, or operational promises that stay blocked until supported by owner-provided facts.
8. Deployment/launch question - any question about preview, provider setup, launch, production, or deployment, which remains separate from content feedback.
9. Out-of-scope request - ecommerce, cart, checkout, payment, purchase, order, booking, reservation, fulfilment, stock-reservation, customer account, public tracking, upload, notification, CRM, outbound messaging, n8n/Pinecone runtime, browser Supabase, or service-role runtime requests.

## What is not feedback evidence

The following must not be treated as owner feedback evidence, owner approval, acceptance evidence, launch evidence, or deployment approval:

- Casual comments outside a captured owner review record.
- Unverified assumptions.
- Codex/agent guesses.
- Inferred business facts.
- Public route tests.
- Local screenshots.
- Preview/deployment output.
- Validator output or passing local tests.
- Admin helper text or local documentation templates.

## Intake guardrails

- Capture the raw owner comment separately before classifying it.
- Keep this document blank of actual owner comments.
- Do not infer public business facts from design, screenshots, existing placeholder text, or agent assumptions.
- Do not convert a deployment/launch question into deployment approval.
- Do not convert a content correction into owner approval or sign-off.
- Keep public routes rental/enquiry-only and non-promissory.
- Keep protected admin review helpers out of public source and public routes.
- Keep evidence status as [NOT EVIDENCE / NOT RECORDED].
- Keep deployment status as [DEPLOYMENT APPROVAL: NOT GRANTED].
