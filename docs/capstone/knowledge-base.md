## Delivery and Pickup

Delivery can be arranged for Singapore venues, subject to schedule, access, and item availability. Customers should provide venue name, hall or booth number, loading bay details, allowed delivery window, lift access, security pass requirements, setup window, dismantle window, and onsite contact person.

For event or exhibition furniture delivery arrangements, the draft website terms request at least 5 working days' notice. For large exhibitions, peak periods, custom requirements, or venues with strict access rules, earlier notice is strongly recommended.

Standard delivery and collection timing in the draft terms is Monday to Saturday, 10:00am to 6:00pm, excluding public holidays, unless otherwise confirmed in writing.

After-hours, Sunday, public holiday, or venue-specific timings may be subject to additional charges and manpower availability.
