# SpaceKonceptRental Rental Terms

## Website Use

Information submitted through the website, enquiry forms, WhatsApp, email, or
other communication channels may be used to respond to enquiries, prepare
quotations, process rental requests, improve services, and manage customer
support.

General feedback, suggestions, ideas, or comments may be treated as
non-confidential except where protected by applicable privacy laws or related
to payment, booking, or personal information.

Website content, product information, layouts, logos, graphics, icons, page
headers, and other website materials are owned by or licensed to SpaceKonceptRental
and/or FJ & Joseph Pte Ltd. Customers may not copy, modify, distribute, publish,
or create derivative works from website content without prior written permission.

## Security And Proper Use

Customers should use the website only for lawful purposes.

Customers should not:

- tamper with, disrupt, or interfere with the website;
- submit false information or fraudulent bookings;
- access systems or accounts without authorisation;
- probe or test website vulnerabilities without permission;
- upload harmful code or spam;
- overload, flood, spam, mailbomb, crash, or disrupt the website;
- impersonate SpaceKonceptRental, FJ & Joseph Pte Ltd, customers, or representatives;
- send unauthorised advertising on behalf of SpaceKonceptRental;
- use robots, scrapers, or automated extraction tools outside normal public
  browser access.

## Delivery Of Event And Exhibition Furniture

Customers should provide at least 5 working days' notice for event or exhibition
furniture delivery arrangements.

Earlier notice is strongly recommended for:

- large exhibitions;
- peak periods;
- custom requirements;
- venues with strict access rules.

Urgent requests may be reviewed, but delivery is subject to item availability,
manpower, venue access, and schedule confirmation.

Standard delivery timing is Monday to Saturday, 10:00am to 6:00pm, excluding
public holidays, unless otherwise confirmed in writing.

Venues such as exhibition halls, shopping malls, hotels, offices, condominiums,
convention centres, and event spaces may have specific delivery slots, loading
bay rules, lift booking requirements, contractor pass requirements, or security
procedures.

Customers are responsible for checking these requirements with venue
management, the event organiser, or official contractor before delivery is
arranged.

Delivery duration depends on quantity, item size, venue access, loading bay
distance, lift availability, booth location, and setup requirements.

## Venue Access And Readiness

Customers must ensure that:

- the delivery location is accessible at the confirmed date and time;
- the loading bay, lift, corridor, booth, or event space is ready;
- required permits, passes, security clearance, lift bookings, and venue
  approvals are arranged in advance;
- someone is present onsite to receive and confirm the furniture;
- the furniture can fit through lifts, doors, staircases, corridors, booth
  entrances, and venue access points.

If delivery cannot be completed because of missing permits, denied access,
unsuitable access route, absent onsite contact, blocked setup area, incorrect
information, or venue restrictions, additional charges may apply for waiting
time, redelivery, rescheduling, manpower, or cancellation.

## Protection Of Property

The delivery team will take reasonable care during delivery and setup.

Customers should inform SpaceKonceptRental in advance about fragile flooring,
walls, fixtures, lifts, narrow access points, or venue protection requirements.

SpaceKonceptRental is not responsible for pre-existing damage, venue
restrictions not disclosed before delivery, or damage caused by third-party
contractors, venue staff, exhibitors, visitors, or event attendees.

## Collection Of Event And Exhibition Furniture

Customers should provide at least 5 working days' notice to arrange collection,
unless collection timing has already been confirmed in the rental agreement or
quotation.

For exhibitions and events, collection is usually subject to the official
dismantling schedule, venue rules, loading bay availability, and event organiser
instructions.

Standard collection timing is Monday to Saturday, 10:00am to 6:00pm, excluding
public holidays, unless otherwise confirmed in writing.

After-hours collection, Sunday collection, public holiday collection, or
venue-specific dismantling timing may be subject to additional charges and
manpower availability.

## Collection Requirements

Customers must ensure that:

- all rented items are ready for collection at the confirmed time;
- the collection location is accessible;
- the furniture is not blocked by booths, displays, stock, rubbish, renovation
  works, or third-party equipment;
- venue passes, lift booking, loading bay access, and security clearance are
  arranged if required;
- an authorised onsite contact is available during collection.

If collection cannot be completed because of access issues, missing approvals,
absent onsite contact, or venue restrictions, additional rental, waiting,
manpower, storage, or recollection charges may apply.

## Condition Upon Collection

All rented items must be returned in the same condition as received, except for
fair wear and tear from normal use.

Customers may be responsible for charges arising from stains, burns, water
damage, breakage, missing parts, loss, misuse, unauthorised movement, or damage
caused by customers, exhibitors, guests, venue staff, or third parties.

## Exchange Policy

For new or confirmed rental items, exchange requests may be reviewed within
7 days of delivery or before the event starts, whichever is earlier.

Exchanges are subject to:

- stock availability;
- delivery schedule;
- item condition;
- venue access;
- any price difference.

The replacement item should be of the same value or lower value. If a
higher-value item is selected, additional rental charges may apply.

Items requested for exchange must be in their original condition.

SpaceKonceptRental may reject an exchange if the item is damaged, stained, used
beyond normal handling, missing parts, or otherwise not in satisfactory
condition.

## Ex-Rental Or Non-New Furniture

Some rental furniture may be ex-rental or previously used. Such items may have
reasonable signs of use, including minor dents, scratches, marks, or wear.

The 7-day exchange policy may not apply to ex-rental, discounted, clearance,
event-use, or specially arranged rental furniture.

## Servicing And Maintenance

During the rental period, if furniture is affected by normal wear and tear,
customers should contact SpaceKonceptRental as soon as possible with photos,
event details, and the rental order reference.

Where appropriate, SpaceKonceptRental may arrange repair, servicing, or
replacement, subject to availability, access, event timing, and the nature of
the issue.

Replacement items may be ex-rental or equivalent items and may not be identical
in colour, finish, model, or condition.

Servicing and maintenance do not cover damage caused by misuse, negligence,
unauthorised relocation, improper handling, water exposure, food or drink
stains, burns, overloading, outdoor exposure without approval, or damage caused
by third parties.
