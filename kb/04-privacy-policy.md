# SpaceKonceptRental Privacy Policy

## Overview

SpaceKonceptRental, a brand operated by FJ & Joseph Pte Ltd, collects, uses,
discloses, stores, and protects personal data collected through the website,
enquiry forms, WhatsApp, email, events, rental enquiries, purchases, and related
services.

The policy explains how customer personal data is handled and how customers may
exercise privacy-related rights.

## Information Collected

SpaceKonceptRental may collect and process information provided through website
forms, quotations, appointment requests, event or exhibition rental enquiries,
orders, floor plans, and website support requests.

Collected information may include:

- name;
- company name;
- contact number;
- email address;
- billing address;
- delivery address;
- venue details;
- event details;
- booth details;
- rental requirements;
- correspondence records;
- messages;
- email records;
- call notes;
- attachments;
- photos;
- floor plans;
- service-related communications;
- transaction and fulfilment details;
- delivery, installation, rental, collection, servicing, payment, and support
  details;
- website traffic, location, logs, pages viewed, resources accessed, and other
  communication data;
- event, exhibition, showroom visit, or rental project details.

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purpose it was
collected, or as required for legal, accounting, business, operational,
dispute-resolution, or regulatory purposes.

## Use Of Information

Personal data may be used to:

- present website content effectively;
- respond to enquiries;
- prepare quotations;
- arrange showroom appointments;
- process purchases;
- handle event and exhibition furniture rental requests;
- provide customer support;
- send requested alerts, newsletters, product information, promotional updates,
  appointment reminders, order updates, or delivery updates;
- carry out contracts, quotations, purchase orders, rental agreements,
  installation arrangements, delivery arrangements, or service agreements;
- manage payment, billing, fulfilment, delivery, installation, rental,
  collection, servicing, warranty, maintenance, after-sales support, and
  customer relationship records;
- handle legal proceedings, debt recovery, dispute resolution, audit,
  compliance, professional advice, insurance, and regulatory matters;
- research, design, launch, improve, or promote products, services, seminars,
  events, forums, exhibitions, workshops, or related business activities.

## Direct Marketing

SpaceKonceptRental may use personal data for direct marketing where permitted
by law and where required consent has been obtained, or where the customer has
not objected.

Marketing may cover SpaceKonceptRental, FJ & Joseph Pte Ltd, affiliates,
partners, rewards, loyalty programmes, promotional offers, campaigns, vouchers,
launches, events, seminars, webinars, conferences, product demonstrations,
showroom events, exhibitions, trade shows, and roadshows.

Marketing channels may include email, telephone, WhatsApp, SMS, direct mail,
online advertising, social media, or other communication channels.

Customers may opt out by informing SpaceKonceptRental through the listed contact
details.

## Disclosure Of Information

Personal data may be shared where necessary with:

- personnel, employees, agents, advisers, auditors, contractors, installers,
  delivery teams, event support teams, financial institutions, payment
  processors, insurers, professional advisers, and service providers;
- offices, affiliates, business partners, vendors, suppliers, logistics
  partners, venue partners, exhibition contractors, and relevant counterparts;
- persons under a duty of confidentiality;
- public authorities, regulators, law enforcement agencies, courts, tribunals,
  or other parties where required or permitted by law;
- third parties connected with a merger, restructuring, sale, transfer,
  financing, or acquisition of the business, subject to appropriate safeguards.

## Event Photography And Recording

Customers may be photographed, audio-recorded, or video-recorded at events,
exhibitions, showroom activities, trade shows, launches, seminars, workshops,
or other activities organised, hosted, supported, or attended by
SpaceKonceptRental.

Such recordings may be used for news, webcasts, promotional purposes,
telecasts, advertising, social media, website content, marketing materials,
internal documentation, or other reasonable business purposes, to the extent
permitted by law.

## Cookies

The website may use cookies to distinguish users, support website operation,
improve user experience, analyse website use, and make content or advertising
more relevant.

Cookie categories may include:

- strictly necessary cookies;
- analytical or performance cookies;
- functionality cookies;
- targeting cookies.

Third parties such as advertising networks, analytics providers, payment
providers, social media platforms, embedded content providers, and external
service providers may also use cookies.

The policy references Google Analytics and Google's privacy policy resources.

Customers can block cookies in their browser, but blocking all cookies may
prevent access to some parts of the website.

## Security

SpaceKonceptRental uses reasonable administrative, technical, and physical
safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorised access, collection,
use, disclosure, copying, modification, disposal, or similar risks.

Payment transactions may be encrypted using SSL technology or handled through
secure third-party payment processors.

Internet transmission is not completely secure, and data transmission through
the website or electronic channels is at the customer's own risk.

## Data Transfers

Personal data may be held, processed, or transferred on servers in Singapore
and in other territories where service providers, website providers, cloud
providers, payment processors, communication platforms, analytics providers, or
business partners operate.

Where personal data is transferred overseas, SpaceKonceptRental will take steps
required by applicable law to ensure the transferred personal data receives a
standard of protection comparable to Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act
2012, where applicable.

## Consent And Rights

By using services, submitting enquiries, placing orders, entering rental
arrangements, attending events, or visiting the website, customers consent to
the collection, use, disclosure, and processing of information as outlined in
the policy.

Under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012, individuals may have the
right to:

- check whether SpaceKonceptRental holds personal data about them;
- request access to such data;
- request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data;
- ask about policies and practices relating to personal data;
- withdraw consent for marketing use.

Customers may submit opt-out, access, correction, withdrawal, policy, or
complaint requests to `info@fjnjoseph.com`.

SpaceKonceptRental may need to verify the customer's identity before processing
access, correction, withdrawal, or complaint requests.

## Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, customers should contact SpaceKonceptRental
through the official contact details listed on the website or email
`info@fjnjoseph.com`.
