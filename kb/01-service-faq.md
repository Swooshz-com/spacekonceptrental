# SpaceKonceptRental Service FAQ

## Service Scope

SpaceKonceptRental helps exhibitors, event organisers, agencies, and corporate
teams furnish temporary spaces such as booths, roadshows, launches, pop-ups,
registration areas, hospitality areas, visitor discussion corners, meeting
corners, and compact event spaces.

Furniture availability depends on event date, venue, quantity, and stock.

Common rental requests include:

- tables;
- chairs;
- stools;
- lounge seating;
- sofas;
- ottomans;
- cabinets and storage;
- counters;
- display pieces;
- multifunctional furniture.

SpaceKonceptRental can support exhibition booths, corporate events, roadshows,
launches, brand activations, pop-ups, visitor seating, display zones, meeting
spaces, registration counters, and temporary commercial setups.

For compact booths or small event spaces, SpaceKonceptRental can review the
layout and recommend practical seating, display, and storage options.

## Quote Requests

Customers should send these details for a faster quotation:

- event date;
- venue name;
- hall, booth, or floor location;
- setup timing;
- dismantle or collection timing;
- item list;
- quantity;
- booth size or floor size;
- delivery access details;
- loading bay details;
- reference photos, if available;
- onsite contact person;
- customer name and contact details.

If a customer does not know what furniture they need, collect the event type,
expected guest flow, venue, booth size, and purpose of the area. SpaceKonceptRental
can suggest a practical furniture mix after reviewing those details.

Customers may send tentative details before a venue is confirmed. Final pricing
and delivery arrangements may change once the venue, access timing, and setup
requirements are confirmed.

## Booking Timeline

Customers should book as early as possible, especially for exhibitions, peak
seasons, and large quantities.

Urgent requests can be reviewed, but stock and delivery slots may be limited.

## Pricing And Payment

Rental pricing depends on:

- item type;
- quantity;
- rental duration;
- delivery location;
- setup requirements;
- availability.

Customers should send their event details so the team can quote accurately.

The quotation should state whether delivery, setup, dismantling, collection,
staircase carry-up, after-hours delivery, or special venue handling is included.
Customers should confirm these items before payment.

Payment terms and deposit requirements should be confirmed in the quotation or
rental agreement.

## Delivery, Setup, And Venue Access

Delivery can be arranged for Singapore venues, subject to schedule, venue
access, manpower, item availability, and confirmed arrangements.

Customers should provide:

- venue address;
- hall or booth number;
- loading bay instructions;
- lift access details;
- security pass requirements;
- setup window;
- dismantle window;
- onsite contact person.

Setup can be arranged where applicable. Customers should confirm whether their
quote includes placement, assembly, dismantling, and collection.

Self-collection may be possible for selected items, subject to approval, item
size, stock readiness, and return condition. Customers should confirm before
visiting.

If the venue has strict loading bay, lift booking, contractor pass, or security
rules, customers should send those requirements before delivery is planned.

After-hours delivery may be reviewed depending on manpower, venue access, and
schedule. Extra charges may apply if confirmed.

## Product Selection And Styling

Customers can share a theme, colour palette, booth design, moodboard, or
reference image. The team can recommend available furniture that best fits the
look and function.

A typical exhibition booth may need visitor chairs, discussion tables, storage,
a display counter, and compact seating. The best mix depends on booth size,
traffic flow, product display, and meeting needs.

Lounge furniture for VIP or hospitality areas may be available, subject to
stock. Customers should provide guest count, space size, event style, and
reference images.

Custom or made-to-order solutions may be possible, but they require more lead
time and a separate quote. Customers should provide drawings, dimensions,
materials, and deadline for review.

If an item is unavailable, SpaceKonceptRental can suggest the closest available
alternatives based on size, function, style, and event requirements.

## Changes, Cancellations, Damage, And Issues

Changes and cancellations depend on the confirmed rental terms, event date,
preparation work, and delivery schedule. Customers should contact the team as
early as possible.

Rented items should be returned in the same condition. Damage, stains, missing
items, or loss may result in additional charges based on the rental agreement.

If furniture delivered is wrong or defective, customers should inform the
onsite contact or SpaceKonceptRental team immediately with photos and order
details. The team will review the issue and advise the next step.

Rental extensions may be possible if the items are not reserved for another
booking. Customers should contact the team early to confirm availability and
additional charges.

Disposal services for event waste or third-party items are not currently
confirmed. Customers should provide details of what needs to be removed so the
team can review the request.

## Contact And Viewing

Customers should use the website contact form, email, or WhatsApp shown on the
official contact page.

Selected items may be available for viewing by appointment. Rental item viewing
should be confirmed with the team first.

## Service Limits

SpaceKonceptRental can collect event details and pass them to the team.

Live stock confirmation depends on current inventory availability and should
not be promised unless confirmed by the team.

For urgent requests, collect event date, venue, setup time, item list, and
contact number, then escalate for availability and delivery review.

An official quotation should be issued or approved by the SpaceKonceptRental
team.
