Our Story

Born from a desire for inspired event environments, Space Koncept Rental was established to bridge the gap between high-end interior design and temporary installations. We recognized a need for pieces that carry weight, texture, and history.

What started as a modest collection of mid-century original chairs has grown into a comprehensive portfolio of architectural furniture. Every piece in our curation is selected for its structural integrity, aesthetic purity, and ability to transform a void into a destination.
